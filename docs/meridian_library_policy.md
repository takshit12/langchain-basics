# Meridian Falls Public Library — Patron Policy Handbook

*For circulation desk staff and self-service kiosk reference.*

## Hours

The Meridian Falls Public Library main branch is open:

- Monday–Thursday: 9:00 AM – 8:30 PM
- Friday: 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM
- Saturday: 10:00 AM – 5:00 PM
- Sunday: 1:00 PM – 5:00 PM (Reading Room only; circulation desk closed)

The library is closed on all federal holidays, plus one additional day each
year for "Founder's Day" (the third Monday in October, commemorating the
library's 1953 founding by local timber magnate Eleanor Voss).

## Borrowing Limits & Late Fees

- Standard patrons may borrow up to **25 items** at a time.
- Books and audiobooks: **21-day** loan period, renewable twice online.
- DVDs and board games: **7-day** loan period, renewable once in person only.
- Reference materials, local history archives, and anything marked
  "Voss Collection" may not leave the building.

Late fees are **$0.10 per day per item**, capped at **$5.00 per item**. A
patron's card is automatically suspended once their combined outstanding
fees reach **$15.00**, until the balance is paid or reduced below that
threshold.

## Card Replacement

A first replacement library card is free. Each subsequent replacement in the
same calendar year costs **$2.00**. Patrons under 16 do not need a photo ID
to apply for a card, but do need a parent or guardian co-signature.

## Wifi & Study Rooms

Guest wifi sessions are limited to **4 hours per device per day**. The three
group study rooms on the second floor can be reserved up to **14 days** in
advance, for a maximum of **2 hours per booking**, and no more than **twice
per week** per patron, to keep the rooms available for everyone.
